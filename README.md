# Link Bio Hero

**Your ultimate Instagram bio link companion.**

Link Bio Hero is a simple, customizable landing page builder optimized for Instagram bio links. Showcase multiple links, social media profiles, and call-to-actions in an aesthetically pleasing way without the complexity or cost of existing solutions.

Designed for small business owners, influencers, and content creators on Instagram (under 10k followers) seeking a professional online presence right from their bio.

**Features:**
*   **Easy Drag-and-Drop Editor:** Quickly add and arrange links, text, and images.
*   **Clean Templates:** Choose from pre-designed, aesthetically pleasing layouts.
*   **Social & CTA Focus:** Drive engagement to all your platforms.
*   **Affordable:** Professional results without breaking the bank.
